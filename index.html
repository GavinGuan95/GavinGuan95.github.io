About Me

Hi I'm Yushi Guan. I am a ECE Master of Engineering student focusing on Machine Learning applications, while working full-time as a Deep Learning Acceleration Engineer at Intel Programmable Solutions Group. I work with Prof. Roman Genov on Synthetic Seizure Dataset Augmentation with Wasserstein GANs for my Master of Engineering project. Completing my M.Eng. degree requirement in 2 and a half year while working full-time at Intel, I am a motivated student with strong time management skills. I have solid understanding and hands-on experience within machine learning for Medical Data Augmentation, Natural Language Processing, Visual Question Answering and Neural Network Security.
